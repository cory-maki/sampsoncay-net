Sampson Cay

Responsible Luxury Development · Rosewood Exuma

Sampson Cay is an ultra-luxury private island development in the Exumas, The Bahamas, developed by Yntegra in partnership with Rosewood Hotels & Resorts. A benchmark project in responsible development, environmental stewardship, and sustainable luxury for the Caribbean.

🌏 Exuma, The Bahamas

The Project

A New Standard for Private Island Development

Sampson Cay is the flagship project of Yntegra, a luxury real estate development and investment firm committed to creating exceptional island destinations. Set within one of the most pristine natural environments in the world, Sampson Cay has been conceived as a low-density, thoughtfully curated retreat for discerning global travelers and residents who value privacy, authenticity, and understated elegance.

The development is anchored by Rosewood Hotels & Resorts, one of the world's most prestigious ultra-luxury hotel brands and a global leader in sustainability. Together, Yntegra and Rosewood are creating a destination that preserves the Exumas' extraordinary marine and terrestrial ecosystems while delivering world-class hospitality.

From the outset, Sampson Cay has engaged leading international consultants and specialist agencies to ensure every design, infrastructure, and operational decision reflects the project's commitment to responsible development and long-term environmental stewardship.
