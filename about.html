Responsible Development

A Flagship Project Built on Principled Foundations

Sampson Cay is the flagship project of Yntegra, a luxury real estate development and investment firm dedicated to creating exceptional island destinations. Located in the Exumas, The Bahamas, Sampson Cay has been designed from the ground up as a model of responsible development — one that integrates environmentally conscious planning, international best practices, and a long-term view of both place and community.

From the project's earliest stages, Yntegra engaged leading international consultants, designers, and specialist agencies to ensure that every decision — from site planning and architectural design to infrastructure and materials selection — reflects a commitment to environmental integrity. Sampson Cay is not simply a luxury destination; it is a deliberate exercise in building thoughtfully within one of the world's most pristine natural environments. Follow the project's progress on Yntegra's Instagram for ongoing development updates.

Sustainable Luxury

Where Restraint and Excellence Converge

Sampson Cay has been conceived as a low-density, thoughtfully curated island retreat for discerning global travelers and residents who seek privacy, authenticity, and understated elegance. The development program includes a Rosewood-branded resort, exclusive branded residences, and a carefully selected collection of lifestyle and wellness amenities — among them a refined beach club, destination dining concepts, holistic wellness facilities, and marina infrastructure serving the international yachting community.

Sustainability principles are integrated throughout the design and operations of Sampson Cay. The project emphasizes renewable energy solutions, responsible water management, and land-use planning that prioritizes the preservation of the island's natural character. This approach reflects a fundamental belief that excellence in luxury and excellence in environmental stewardship are not in tension — they reinforce one another. Trusted Global Partnerships

Anchored by Rosewood Hotels & Resorts

The Sampson Cay development is anchored by Rosewood Hotels & Resorts, one of the world's most prestigious ultra-luxury hotel brands. Rosewood's philosophy — A Sense of Place® — guides its portfolio of award-winning properties across the globe and is central to the vision for Sampson Cay. Every element of the guest and resident experience is being designed in alignment with Rosewood's service ethos: deeply personal, culturally connected, and rooted in the spirit of the destination.

Rosewood is also globally recognized for its leadership in sustainability, having received numerous international sustainability awards. This partnership brings together Yntegra's development expertise and Rosewood's operational excellence to create a destination that sets a new benchmark for sustainable luxury hospitality — not only in the Caribbean, but internationally. Environmental Stewardship

Preserving the Exumas for Future Generations

The Exumas are among the most ecologically significant archipelagos in the Caribbean, home to exceptional marine biodiversity, pristine coral reefs, and rare terrestrial ecosystems. Sampson Cay's development strategy is built around the preservation of these resources. Yntegra has worked with international environmental specialists to develop a framework for responsible land use, marine protection, and ecosystem management that will guide the project through construction and into long-term operations.

Innovative sustainable practices span every area of the development — from building design that minimizes environmental footprint, to water systems that reduce consumption and protect the surrounding marine environment. Sampson Cay's approach to environmental stewardship goes beyond compliance; it reflects a deep respect for the natural landscape that makes the Exumas extraordinary. Explore the project's sustainable design approach in the Exumas for further detail.

Economic Empowerment

Creating Lasting Value for Bahamian Communities

Beyond its physical development, Sampson Cay is designed to generate meaningful and lasting socio-economic value for The Bahamas. Yntegra is committed to working closely with Bahamian stakeholders throughout the Exumas — creating employment opportunities across construction and operations phases, engaging local and national suppliers, and fostering genuine community partnerships that extend the project's positive impact well beyond the island itself.

This integrated approach to economic empowerment is not an afterthought; it is a core design principle of Sampson Cay. The project's long-term success is understood to be inseparable from the prosperity of the communities it operates within.
